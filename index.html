I am Rafsanur Rahman Rakib — a Junior Associate Lawyer at The Legal Solution & Research Associate (LSRA), Chattogram, and a current LL.M. candidate at Port City International University. My practice centres on property document vetting, title examination, banking security instruments, and the preparation of legal opinions for leading financial institutions and real estate companies across Bangladesh.

Beyond the courtroom and the chamber, I am a published academic author — my research on international institutions and human rights in a multipolar world appears in the Journal of Tianjin University Science and Technology (Vol. 58(11), 2025). I am a national moot court competitor who earned 3rd Best Memorial at the Bangladesh National Round of the Stetson International Environmental Moot Court Competition 2025, and a decorated university debater. I believe the law's greatest power lies in the intersection of rigorous scholarship, persuasive advocacy, and ethical practice.
